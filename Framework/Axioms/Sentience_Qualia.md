#### Procedure

2. **Luminosity Variation**:

3. **Color Blindness Simulation**:
   - Apply randomized weights to the saturation of color channels for each square in a subset of trials, with weights ranging from 0 (complete greyscale, simulating full color blindness) to 1 (full color).

   - Participants are asked to identify the red squares, noting any differences in shade or intensity.

5. **Data Collection**:
